1. Who We Are

RoofCallNow ("we," "us," or "our") is a lead generation service that connects homeowners with local roofing contractors. We operate this website to collect consumer inquiries and match them with service providers.

2. Information We Collect

When you use our website or submit a request, we may collect:

  • Contact information such as name, phone number, and email address
  • Location information including ZIP code, city, and state
  • Property information such as home size, roof age, and damage type
  • Device and browser information including IP address, browser type, and operating system
  • Usage data such as pages visited, time on site, and clicks

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Connect you with roofing contractors and home services providers in your area
  • Personalize your experience on our website based on your location and device
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Sharing of Your Information

RoofCallNow operates an inbound call service. When you call the number on our Site, you are connected directly to a roofing contractor. We do not sell your phone number for outbound marketing. Any information you voluntarily submit through our estimate tool may be used to improve your experience on the Site.

We may share your information with:

  • Service providers — companies that help us operate our website, analytics, and call routing infrastructure

We do not sell your personal information to third-party marketers, insurance companies, or financial institutions.

5. Your Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:
